What Are Supper Clubs...

"An underground restaurant, sometimes known as a supper club or closed door restaurant, is a social dining restaurant operated out of someone's home. They are usually advertised by word of mouth."

The origin of supper clubs is unknown, however, most believe that it was first stumbled across by the western world in Cuba in the early 2000's. The concept was brought over to the UK before exploding in popularity in countries such as South Africa and the Netherlands.

Cook House believe that the best friendships are made over sharing a good meal, and with today's amateur culinary world exploding we wanted to create a platform that would allow foodies alike to eat, drink and laugh together.

Hosts...

We would love all of our users to try out hosting as we believe it to be one of the most rewarding experiences. Once registered follow the Create Event link and here you can advertise your Supper Club to our registered users.

It's simple and you can add as much information as you choose, we do however require an event name, location, cuisine, date, menu and any additional extras. Your menu is up to you, as many courses as you'd like, or sharing style anything goes and we've seen it all!

Guests...

Search for supper clubs near and far. Overnight stays, city breaks, weekends by the coast these are all perfect opportunities to experience an incredible evening put on by a local host. You can enjoy world class food without the price tag and meet some truly interesting people.

Once you've chosen a supper club register your interest and make sure to inform the host of any dietary requirements. If you have any questions on the event this is your time to ask the host or us. Don't be afraid to ask we all understand this is a new experience for most.
